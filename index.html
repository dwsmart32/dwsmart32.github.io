Research
NaturalFlow: Reducing Disruptive Pauses for Natural Speech Flow in Simultaneous Speech-to-Speech Translation
Dongwook Lee*, Youngho Cho*, Sangkwon Park, Heeseung Kim, Sungroh Yoon
[arXiv] [Project Page]
Interspeech 2026 (long) Oral

Consecutive translation can speak in complete phrases, but makes listeners wait.
Simultaneous translation starts sooner, but often breaks speech into short bursts with disruptive pauses.
Can we keep simultaneous translation's low latency while making it as easy to follow as consecutive translation?
